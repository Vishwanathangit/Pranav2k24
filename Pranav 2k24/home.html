ABOUT US

MSEC

Meenakshi Sundararajan Engineering College (MSEC), renowned for its academic prowess and engagement in social initiatives, stands as an autonomous institution committed to fostering holistic education. Guided by a vision to cultivate critical thinking and ethical values, MSEC aspires to be a premier "Centre of Excellence." With a focus on technical proficiency and moral integrity, it prepares students to adeptly tackle industry hurdles and positively influence the nation's trajectory.

Prof.K.R Sundararajan

In 1947, Prof. K.R. Sundararajan established the Indian Institute of Engineering Technology (IIET) to address the shortage of engineering colleges, fueling the aspirations of countless young individuals. Later, he founded Meenakshi College for Women in 1974. His vision culminated in the inception of a full-fledged engineering college in July 2001, dedicated to providing quality education to all. Prof. K.R.S.'s legacy continues to inspire generations, shaping the future of engineering education in South India.

Dr. K.S. Babai

Dr. K.S. Babai, who graduated in 1966 from College of Engineering, Guindy (College Of Engineering,Campus-Chennai), did her Masters from IIT Madras in the year 1980 and Ph.D from Annamalai University. She excelled in the field of technical education by leading the Meenakshi Sundararajan Engineering College.She dedicated her life to serving others, educating and uplifting them. She had been a great supporter and never failed to inspire us with her perseverance and willpower.

Mr. N. Sreekanth

Mr. N. Sreekanth, our secretary, fervently advocates for our involvement in diverse programs aimed at enhancing our skill repertoire, spanning communication, problem-solving, teamwork, and creativity. His proactive role in fostering and nurturing various clubs within the institution is commendable. By prioritizing extracurricular activities, he facilitates a comprehensive learning journey, empowering us to emerge as adept professionals prepared for the challenges of tomorrow.

Dr. S. V. Saravanan

Dr. S. V. Saravanan, our principal, champions the integration of technical skills with academics, nurturing a holistic educational environment. His hands-on approach fosters a supportive atmosphere where students thrive. Through motivational speeches and practical guidance, he ignites a love for learning and growth. Beyond administrative roles, his dedication extends to mentoring students, providing invaluable resources for their success. Dr. Saravanan's commitment to student development ensures a well-rounded educational journey towards excellence.
